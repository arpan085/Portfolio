See more about the project

The main goal was to make premium streaming subscriptions easier and more affordable to discover for users in Nepal.

I built it because the options were scattered and hard to compare, especially for people who wanted something simple and trustworthy.

Along the way, I worked on the layout, user flow, and responsive design so the site would feel clear on both mobile and desktop.

I also learned that good frontend work is often about making fewer choices feel clearer rather than adding more features.
